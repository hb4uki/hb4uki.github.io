What Antarctica Taught Me

"Leadership 3 groups • My first leadership lesson was there are 3 types of overlapping leadership: 1) leading self 2) leading others 3) leading for the greater good • Self- leadership can be defined as identifying and creating a deliberate future, be it for yourself or others. • In order to create change or lead others, self-leadership is critical… And as I’ve heard George Lee Sye say, as it turns out, the hardest person we will ever lead is in fact, our very own self. But by knowing and understanding myself, including my quirks and quarks, I can know and understand others better and am better able to connect with others to have more positive outcomes. • What I like about looking at leadership like this, is that every voice counts. Everyone of us has the power to be a role model and a leader within our circle of influence. • I also realized, that by looking at leadership in this way, ....Change Can Come From Anyone, anywhere.... For me this was an incredibly empowering realization … Change Can Come From Anyone, anywhere."

Heidi
